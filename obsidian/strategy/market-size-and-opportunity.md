# Market Size & Opportunity

## The Problem in Dollars

- **Per practice:** $500K-$1M in unscheduled treatment plans (ADA benchmark)
- **Industry-wide:** 177,559 dental businesses in the US (IBISWorld 2025) = **$89-178B** in idle treatment revenue annually
- **Completion gap:** Treatment plan acceptance is 56%, but completion drops to 46% — a 10% leakage that current tools fail to close

## Why the Money Isn't Being Recovered

Current tools generate call lists for staff to manually work through. No existing product fires autonomous, multi-step outreach sequences without human input. The gap is automation, not awareness.

## Target Buyer Profile

Tech-forward practices on legacy PMS (Open Dental, Dentrix, Eaglesoft) that already layer point solutions — online booking, digital intake, Weave/Lighthouse. These practices have demonstrated willingness to adopt tools and are the fastest-yes customers.
