# Pricing Rationale

> Why $299/month, how it positions against competitors, and the volume/annual pricing strategy.

---

## The Price: $299/month per practice ($3,588/year)

### Why $299

1. **ROI-anchored, not cost-anchored.** A practice recovers ~$150K/year from a $500K backlog. $299/mo is a 42x return. The price doesn't need to feel "cheap" — it needs to feel like an obvious yes relative to the value recovered
2. **Middle of the market.** Full-suite communication platforms (Weave, Solutionreach) run $350-$500+/mo. Retaine undercuts them while delivering more focused value. Niche analytics tools (BlueIQ) run ~$149/mo — Retaine's AI-powered automation justifies the premium over dashboards that just show data
3. **Margin math works.** At $299/mo, gross margin is 57% at 10 practices and 90%+ at 100 practices. At $199, launch margin was only 38% — the $100 increase buys breathing room during the riskiest phase (see [[unit-economics-and-scaling-costs]])
4. **Simple to explain.** Flat monthly fee, per practice. No per-seat, no per-message, no per-provider tiers. Practice owners can budget for it instantly

### Key Positioning Takeaways

- **$299 undercuts every broad platform** (Weave, Solutionreach, RevenueWell, Adit) by $50-$300/mo
- **$299 commands a premium over analytics-only tools** (BlueIQ at $149) — justified by AI automation that actually sends messages, not just reports
- **$299 matches SmileData** — but SmileData surfaces opportunities for staff to act on manually. Retaine fires sequences autonomously
- **No competitor publishes pricing transparently.** Retaine should. Transparent pricing builds trust with practice owners who are tired of "request a demo" gatekeeping

## Annual Pricing Strategy

### Recommended: Annual Discount

| Plan | Monthly | Annual (paid yearly) | Savings |
|------|---------|---------------------|---------|
| Monthly | $299/mo | — | — |
| Annual | — | $249/mo ($2,988/yr) | 2 months free (~17% discount) |

**Why offer annual:**
- Reduces churn — annual contracts create switching cost
- Improves cash flow — full year collected upfront
- Industry norm — Weave, Solutionreach, DI all require annual contracts. Offering it as a *choice* (not a requirement) is a differentiator

**Why also offer monthly:**
- Removes friction for the pilot and early adopters
- Signals confidence — "we don't need to lock you in"
- Practices can upgrade to annual after seeing 60-90 days of results

## Volume Pricing (DSO / Multi-Location)

For the 110-location opportunity and future DSO deals:

| Locations | Per-Location Price | Annual Value | Discount |
|-----------|-------------------|-------------|---------|
| 1-4 | $299/mo | $3,588/yr | — |
| 5-24 | $249/mo | $2,988/yr | ~17% (annual rate) |
| 25-99 | $199/mo | $2,388/yr | ~33% |
| 100+ | Custom | Negotiated | — |

**The 110-location deal at $199/mo = $262K ARR.** At $249/mo = $329K ARR. Worth leaving room to negotiate while still landing above the $199 floor where margins are thinner.

**Important:** Volume pricing should be reserved for committed multi-location contracts (12+ months), not individual practices trying to negotiate down.

## Pricing Objection Responses

### "That's expensive compared to [BlueIQ / cheaper tool]"
"BlueIQ shows you which patients have unscheduled treatment. Retaine actually contacts them — automatically, with AI-personalized messages, across SMS, email, and voicemail. It's the difference between a report and a revenue recovery engine. The $150/mo difference pays for itself with a single recovered filling."

### "Weave does treatment follow-up too, and we already pay for it"
"Weave generates a follow-up list for your staff to work manually. Retaine fires sequences autonomously — no staff action required. Most practices run both: Weave for phones and reviews, Retaine for treatment recovery. The $299/mo is paid for many times over by the revenue it recovers that the Weave call list never gets to."

### "Can you do $199?"
"Our annual plan is $249/mo — that's 2 months free. For multi-location commitments (5+ practices), we have volume pricing. At single-location $299/mo, the product typically recovers $12,500/month in its first 90 days — the ROI is overwhelming at any of these price points."

## Pricing Transparency as Differentiator

Every major competitor hides pricing behind "request a demo" or "contact sales." This is intentional — they price-discriminate by practice size. But it frustrates buyers.

**Retaine should publish pricing on the website.** Benefits:
- Builds trust with practice managers who are tired of opaque pricing
- Self-qualifies leads (no wasted demo calls with practices who can't afford it)
- Supports the PLG motion — prospect sees price, tries sandbox, converts without talking to sales
- Aligns with the "modern SaaS" positioning against legacy competitors
